Here are the rules

Debate Rules - Forensic

a. Forensic Debate is only open to students from the Advanced Speech and Debate class (Trimester 3).

b. Forensic teams are 2 person teams, and are open to 7th/8th grade students.

c. Top team from each round advances

d. Debate topic announced March 1st. Teams must prepare for Affirmative and Negative positions.


Debate Rules - Trivium

a. Trivium Debate is open to teams of 6th grade students and 7th/8th grade students (2 categories).

b. Trivium Debate is open to all. No prerequisites.

c. Teams Must have 5 people. ONE role per person: Presenter, Defender, or Attacker

d. Participants will be able to organize their teams. Organizers do reserve the right to add individual sign-ups to a team with less than 5 people.

e. Rounds consist of 4 teams debating each other.

f. Top 2 two teams advance to elimination rounds.

g. Teams will choose defense topics on April 1st bin a lottery style draw.
